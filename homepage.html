Growth Mindset

Have you ever did something new? Did you fail at it once, twice or even three times? Did you continue despite those failures and along the way change some things up so that one day you will succeed? If you answered yes to this you exemplify the personality attribute of a growth mindset. Growth Mindset is one of 8 BSM's (Behavior, Skills & Mindset). Growth Mindset is a very important attribute to exemplify because it enables us to challenge what we believed to be true and in return discover entirely different positive truths about ourselves.


Mantras of Growth Mindset

  • "It's never to late to learn"
  • "It's ok if I fail, at least I learned something"
  • "I appreciate constructive criticism"
  • "I can always improve in something if I try"

Examples of growth mindset in popular culture include Michael Jordan who was cut from his freshman basketball team. He in turn became one of the greatest basketball players the world has ever seen. Oprah Winfrey being another example. She was demoted from her job as a news anchor because she was told she "wasn't fit for television"
